# FairLens Guardian  
### AI-Powered Responsible Governance Platform for Ethical and Unbiased Decision-Making

## Overview
FairLens Guardian is an AI-powered Responsible AI Governance Platform designed to detect, explain, and mitigate bias in automated decision-making systems.

The platform combines fairness auditing, Explainable AI (XAI), real-time monitoring, and Google Gemini-powered recommendations to promote transparent, ethical, and accountable AI outcomes.

---

## Problem Statement
Automated decision-making systems in hiring, finance, healthcare, and public services can inherit hidden bias, resulting in unfair and discriminatory outcomes.

FairLens Guardian addresses this challenge by enabling proactive bias detection, fairness monitoring, and compliance-ready governance.

---

## Solution
Our solution provides:

- Bias detection in datasets and models  
- Real-time fairness monitoring  
- Explainable AI insights  
- Gemini-powered ethical recommendations  
- Bias mitigation support  
- Compliance audit report generation  

---

## Key Features
### Bias Detection Engine
Detects hidden bias in data and models.

### Fairness Monitoring Dashboard
Tracks fairness metrics continuously.

### Explainable AI (XAI)
Provides transparent reasoning behind decisions.

### Gemini Ethics Copilot
Generates intelligent bias mitigation recommendations.

### Drift & Bias Alerts
Identifies fairness degradation in real time.

### Compliance Reports
Produces ethical AI governance reports.
